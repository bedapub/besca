Besca standard worklow offers a standardized series of steps from raw-data to filtered one in order to start the annotation processes.

Besca is distributed with such workflow available as a notebook: https://github.com/bedapub/besca/blob/master/workbooks/standard_workflow_besca2.ipynb

The differents individuals steps are described in the tutorial: data processing
